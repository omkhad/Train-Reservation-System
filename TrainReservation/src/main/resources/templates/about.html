About Indian Railways

Indian Railways is one of the world's largest railway networks, spanning across the length and breadth of the country. Established in 1853, Indian Railways has played a crucial role in connecting people, cultures, and industries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, efficient, and accessible rail transportation for all. We are committed to modernizing our infrastructure, enhancing passenger experience, and promoting sustainable practices.

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1853
  • Network Length: Over 67,000 kilometers
  • Passenger Carrying Capacity: Millions daily
  • Employees: Over a million
